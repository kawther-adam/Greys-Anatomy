Grey's Anatomy

Grey's anatomy is a medical based drama that focuses on the life surgical interns and their supervisors. The drama is mainly centred around Meredith Grey, an aspiring surgeon. She is the daughter of Ellis Grey a renowned general surgeon.

Some fun facts about Grey's Anatomy:

  • Meredith Grey initially started of as a Neuro surgeon and then later specialised in General surgery like her mother
  • There are 18 season's
  • only three of the season 1 cast are still there
